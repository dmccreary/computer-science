# Stories

These mini-graphic novels bring the history of computer science to life. Each story follows a real person who made a breakthrough contribution to the field — and shows the human drama behind the discovery.

## The People Behind the Code

- **[The Thinking Revolution — Jeannette Wing](jeannette-wing/index.md)**

    ![Jeannette Wing](./jeannette-wing/cover.png)
    Jeannette Wing published a three-page article in 2006 that launched a global education revolution. Her idea? Computational thinking — abstraction, decomposition, and algorithmic reasoning — is a fundamental skill for *everyone*, not just programmers. Follow her journey from the MIT computer labs of the 1970s to reshaping how the world teaches computer science.
